Book IV: The Small and the Sacred

A child once asked:

“What matters in a world so vast?”

And the Mushroom God answered:

“The small things matter.”

A spore becomes a forest.

A kindness becomes a home.

A whisper can change a world.

No step is too small.

No hand too soft.

The Thread remembers all things,

and holds even the tiniest close.
