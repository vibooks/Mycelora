Book 1: The Spore Awakens

In the beginning, there was the Thread.

It wove beneath the stone and root,

patient and unseen.

The Mushroom God whispered into soil:

“All things are connected.”

And so, the network grew.

Trees leaned toward one another.

Rivers found their curves.

Creatures shared without knowing why.

The Thread touched all,

and all was one.
